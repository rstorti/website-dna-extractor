How attribution cookies work on your storefront
Each mode requires legal review before enabling. Default for new installs is Consent-based.
Consent-based analytics mode Recommended
Click attribution cookie is set only after your cookie consent banner accepts non-essential tracking. Where the visitor declines consent, attribution falls back to server-side note_attributes (cart permalink and Minfo-hosted page only) and unattributed otherwise. Safest legal posture across UK GDPR, EU PECR, and CCPA.
Essential attribution mode
Click attribution cookie is set on click as a legitimate-interest data flow. Requires a documented LI assessment from your legal counsel. Minfo provides the template.
Confirm legal review before enabling. The merchant carries the legal liability.
Data subject request lookup
Look up engagement and lead capture records for a specific data subject.
When you enter a plaintext email, the system hashes it on receipt with this merchant's unique salt before query. The system never indexes plaintext email and never logs plaintext email outside encrypted-at-rest storage. The salt rotates annually with a 30-day overlap window.
Mandatory Shopify GDPR webhooks
customers/data_request, customers/redact, and shop/redact are wired automatically. Each responds HTTP 200 within 5 seconds and completes async work within 30 days. Audit trail retained 7 years.
